# Refined Heritage

## Visual Narrative
This design system bridges the gap between traditional editorial elegance and modern functional clarity. It is anchored by a deep, sophisticated burgundy and supported by a warm, organic palette that evokes a sense of established trust and curated quality. The interplay of a classic serif headline font with a high-performance sans-serif body typeface creates a hierarchy that feels both prestigious and accessible.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Deep Burgundy - #9D2933):** Used for key brand moments and primary interactive elements, providing a strong, authoritative presence.
- **Secondary (Dusty Rose - #D4A5A5):** A soft, muted tone for supporting elements and subtle UI accents.
- **Tertiary (Coffee Brown - #6D4C41):** An earthy anchor used for badges, decorative details, or high-contrast callouts.
- **Neutral (Warm Cream - #FAF7F2):** A gentle, off-white foundation that provides warmth and reduces eye strain compared to pure white backgrounds.

## Typography
- **Headlines:** *Newsreader* — A versatile serif designed for readability and an editorial, high-end feel.
- **Body & Labels:** *Plus Jakarta Sans* — A modern, geometric sans-serif that ensures high legibility and a contemporary edge in functional text blocks and navigation.

## Structural Foundation
- **Roundedness (2):** Corners feature a moderate radius, striking a balance between professional precision and approachable softness.
- **Spacing (2):** The layout utilizes a standard density. It feels balanced and organized—efficient enough for information-rich interfaces while maintaining clear visual breathing room between components.
